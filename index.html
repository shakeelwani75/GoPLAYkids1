Welcome to GoPLAYkids

Enjoy safe and fun play areas for kids!

We are proud to be the first indoor educational play area opened in Swansgate shopping centre. We have a unique approach to education and play which your little ones will love. The incredible creative role play area is designed so your little one can open their imagination and lose themselves in a world of their own. Child development is Key at GoPLAY kids this is why we have a creative and Lego and Art area for your little ones to develop their problem-solving awareness and express their colourful self with the Art. There is Also an onsite Cafe where you can relax and enjoy your coffee and cakes ,all whilst watching your loved ones explore and play in a safe and creative environment.
